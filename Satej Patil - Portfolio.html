About

I'm an AI product manager who builds. Three years shipping AI features — RAG pipelines, LLM workflows, onboarding flows — most recently as the sole PM at an early-stage fintech AI startup, where I owned product end to end.

I don't just write specs and hand them off. I prototype, I publish my own tooling, and I own the number the product is supposed to move. The best AI PMs do judgment work, not translation work: anyone can relay a feature request, but the hard part is deciding what's worth building when the model can do almost anything — and being honest about what it can't.
